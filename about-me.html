About Me

Founder

Poonam Rajput

Poonam Rajput Upadhyay, our esteemed founder, is a distinguished figure in the luxury wedding planning industry. With over a decade of experience orchestrating bespoke weddings, Poonam has become synonymous with meticulously crafted celebrations that resonate with elegance and personal touch.

Her journey in the event planning realm began from a passion for uniting people in celebration and an acute attention to detail. Believing that every wedding should reflect the individuality of the couple, she founded Shubh Aarambh Events to set a new standard in luxury wedding experiences across Asia.

Poonam is known for her deep understanding of various cultural nuances and an unyielding commitment to making each wedding a unique personal event.
